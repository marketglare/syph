# syph

This is a simulator of Syphilis, a disease caused by the division of Treponema Pallidum bacteria.
Objective: Defeat Treponema by eliminating the infectious agent.

Rules:
The pathogen divides every 30 seconds (equivalent to a real 24-hour period).
Pressing the "Kill" button simulates the action of an Antibiotic, which attacks Treponema with a 70-95% chance of killing.

There is a 30% chance that, upon sensing the risk, the bacteria may enter a dormant "L-form", ceasing to reproduce and becoming invulnerable.
Release from the dormant phase is random, within 1-10 in-game days (30 seconds-3 minutes).

Win condition: all treponemas are defeated. 
Defeat condition: the treponema has multiplied more than 1000 units.
